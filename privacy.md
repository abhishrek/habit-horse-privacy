# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 31-Dec-2026

Habit Horse (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Habit Horse collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

Habit Horse collects only the information necessary to provide its core functionality.

### Phone Number
- We collect your **phone number** solely to create and identify your account.
- The phone number is used for secure authentication and account management.
- Your phone number is **not used for marketing, advertising, or tracking purposes**.

### Habit Data
- The habits, progress, and related data you create in Habit Horse are associated with your account.
- This data is used only to provide habit-tracking functionality.

### Analytics and Tracking
- Habit Horse does **not** use third-party advertising SDKs.
- Habit Horse does **not** track users across apps or websites.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Habit Horse uses your information only to:
- Authenticate and manage your account
- Maintain and sync your habit data
- Provide core app functionality

We do **not** use your phone number for advertising, marketing, or promotional communications.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Your phone number and habit data are stored securely using industry-standard security practices.
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized systems only.
- Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 4. Data Sharing

Habit Horse does **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data may be disclosed only if required to:
- Comply with applicable laws or legal obligations
- Protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the App

## 5. Data Retention

- Your data is retained only for as long as your account remains active.
- When you delete your account, your phone number and associated habit data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable time frame, unless retention is required by law.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Delete your account at any time from within Habit Horse

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Habit Horse is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of Habit Horse after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
